#+OPTIONS: toc:nil
#+TITLE: RNA Extraction with Spin Columns
* Reagents
- RNeasy® Mini Kit (Qiagen Cat# 74106).
- *Buffer RLT* (add β-mercaptoethanol at 10 µL per 1 mL if working with RNase-rich tissues): 350 µL per sample.
- *70% ethanol* prepared with RNase-free H₂O: 350 µL per sample.
- *Buffer RPE*: Add ethanol as directed when opening a new kit.
  
* Note
- A 6-well plate is usually used for /in vitro/ experiments, with
  200,000–300,000 cells per well.

* Procedure
1. Add 350 µL Buffer RLT to the cell culture plate and scrape off the
   cells.
2. Transfer the lysate to a QIAshredder column (Qiagen Cat# 79656).
3. Centrifuge at 20,000 × g for 2 minutes and collect the
   flow-through.
4. Transfer the lysate to a gDNA Eliminator column and centrifuge at
   8,000 × g for 1 minute. Keep the flow-through in the collection
   tube and discard the column.
5. Add 350 µL of 70% ethanol and mix thoroughly by pipetting.
6. Transfer up to 700 µL of sample to an RNeasy Mini spin column.
7. Centrifuge at 8,000 × g for 15 seconds. Discard the flow-through.
8. Add 700 µL Buffer RW1. Centrifuge at 8,000 × g for 15 seconds and
   discard the flow-through.
9. Add 500 µL Buffer RPE. Centrifuge at 8,000 × g for 15 seconds and
   discard the flow-through.
10. Add another 500 µL Buffer RPE. Centrifuge at 8,000 × g for 2
    minutes.
11. Without disturbing the flow-through, place the column into a new 2
    mL collection tube and centrifuge at 20,000 × g for 1 minute to
    dry the membrane.
12. Transfer the column to a new 1.5 mL microcentrifuge tube. Add 30
    µL RNase-free water directly to the membrane and centrifuge at
    8,000 × g for 1 minute.
13. Collect and label the tubes.
14. Place RNA on ice while preparing for cDNA synthesis, or store at -20°C.
